Common Questions

Does BatchSweep delete photos automatically?
No. BatchSweep only stages photos for review. Photos are deleted only after you confirm the final delete action.

Where are my photos processed?
On your device. BatchSweep does not upload your photos or videos to a server.

What does BatchSweep Pro unlock?
BatchSweep Pro unlocks Stage All Similar, which lets you stage all detected extra copies across similar photo batches faster.

How do I restore my purchase?
Open BatchSweep, go to Settings, and tap Restore Purchases.

How do I manage Photos permission?
Open iOS Settings, find BatchSweep, then update Photos access.
