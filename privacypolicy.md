Privacy Policy for BatchSweep

Effective Date: July 1, 2026

BatchSweep is a photo cleanup app that helps users review similar photos and stage unwanted items before deleting them from Apple Photos.

Information We Collect

BatchSweep does not collect, sell, rent, or share personal data.

Photos and Videos

BatchSweep requests access to your Photos library so it can scan visible photos on your device and help identify similar photo batches. Photo analysis happens on device. BatchSweep does not upload your photos or videos to our servers.

In-App Purchases

BatchSweep Pro is sold through Apple’s App Store using StoreKit. Purchase processing, receipts, and payment information are handled by Apple. BatchSweep does not receive or store your payment card details.

Local App Data

BatchSweep may store local preferences and review state on your device, such as staged or kept item identifiers, theme preference, and scan summary information. This data stays on your device and is used only to provide app functionality.

Data Sharing

BatchSweep does not share your photos, videos, or personal information with third parties.

Analytics and Tracking

BatchSweep does not use third-party advertising trackers and does not track users across apps or websites.

Deleting Photos

BatchSweep cannot delete photos without your confirmation. When you confirm deletion, Apple Photos handles the deletion process and moves deleted items according to iOS Photos behavior.

Children’s Privacy

BatchSweep is not designed to collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If changes are made, the updated policy will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
